About Us

Welcome to the Civic Welfare Training Service Attendance Management System (CWTS-AMS) using RFID. Our system is designed to automate and streamline the attendance tracking process for training sessions organized by the Civic Welfare Training Service. With CWTS-AMS, trainers can efficiently record attendance using RFID technology, eliminating the need for manual attendance sheets and reducing administrative overhead. Learn more about the features, benefits, and development team behind CWTS-AMS.



Instructions

This section provides detailed instructions on how to use the CWTS-AMS effectively. Learn how trainers can log in securely to the system, create and manage training sessions, enroll participants, and record attendance using RFID scanners. Participants can also access instructions on how to register their RFID tags, check-in to training sessions, and view their attendance history. Whether you're a trainer or a participant, our step-by-step instructions will guide you through every aspect of the system.
